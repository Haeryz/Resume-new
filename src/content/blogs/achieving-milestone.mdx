# Building a Research-First Portfolio

This portfolio is being reshaped around the work I want people to inspect first:
research contributions, reproducible AI systems, and engineering projects that
make experiments easier to evaluate.

## Current Direction

My focus is on LLMs, VLMs, reinforcement learning, and retrieval-augmented
generation for practical intelligent systems. The strongest current thread is
legal-domain AI for Indonesian human trafficking jurisprudence.

## What Matters Here

- **Grounded answers**: model output should be traceable to documents and cases.
- **Useful datasets**: research systems need clean source material before clever prompts.
- **Evaluation discipline**: fluent text is not enough without retrieval and citation checks.

## Next

The site will keep evolving as papers, datasets, models, and prototypes mature.
